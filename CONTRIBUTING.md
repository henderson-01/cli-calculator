# 🤝 Contributing to cli-calculator

First off, thank you for considering contributing to **CLI-Calculator**! 🎉 It's people like you that make this project awesome.

## 🛑 Important Note About Merges

Please note that **I am not accepting merge requests to the `main` branch**. 🚫 🔀

This project was created as part of my personal **learning journey** 🎓 🐍. I want to keep the codebase exactly as it is to preserve the lessons I've learned along the way. I appreciate your understanding! 🙏

## 🍴 Fork & Adapt

While I can't accept direct merges, you are **more than welcome** to:

1. **Fork the repository** 🍴
2. **Use it as you please** 🛠️
3. **Adapt it to your needs** 🎨
4. **Build your own version** 🚀

Feel free to tweak the UI, add new operations, or change the logic entirely. This is open-source, after all! 🌍 ✨

## 💡 How to Get Started

1. **Fork** the repo by clicking the "Fork" button at the top right of the page.
2. **Clone** your fork to your local machine.

## 🐛 Found a Bug?

If you find a bug, please feel free to open an **Issue** 🐛. I might reply with suggestions or tips on how to fix it yourself! 💡
